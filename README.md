# Sales Analysis

### Project Overview

A project that is aimed to provide insight into the sales performance of Davies Stores over the past years. By analyzing various aspect of the sales data, we seek to identify trends,
make data driven decisions, and gain a deeper understanding of the company's performance.

### Tool(S)

- PowerBI

### Data Cleaning/Preparation

We performed the following task in the initial data preparation phase:
1. Data loading and inspection.
2. Handling missing values.
3. Data cleaning and formatting.

### Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA)

EDA involves exploing the sales data to answer some key questions, such as:

1. What is the total revenue generated across the two years?
2. What are the months with the highest and lowest revenue generated?
3. What is the performance of our sales reps?
4. How do we perform region-wise in terms of revenue generated?

### Result/Findings

The analysis result are summarized as follows:
- The company's sales steadily increased over the years
- Bob Smith is our most performing sales rep
- We made the most revenue in the southern part of the country
